Vote-ify

My third project at General Assembly was a full MERN stack application. Me and my group decided to go with a community based music playlist. We utilized React, Express, MongoDB Atlass, ANT styling, as well as the Spotify API and an additional packaged that produced the music player. Users can login with their spotify account and from there they will be able to select a playlist and suggest songs they feel would be a good fit. The songs are placed in a queue and if they receive enough likes from the community, it gets added to the list.
