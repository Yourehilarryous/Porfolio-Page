The Maze

This was project 1 at General Assembly, build a simple game using Javascript, HTML, and CSS. I chose to make a straightforward "Choose your own adventure" game that allows the user to choose an item and advance in "The Maze". From there a user will pick of of two path options. They will encounter various monsters and depending on the item they have, the player will either die and have to start over or get past the monster.
