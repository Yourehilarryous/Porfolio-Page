Create A Superhero

Project 2 at General Assembly. We were to create an app that utilized MongoDB, Express, and EJS with full CRUD functionality. I worked with my group that allow for users to create a hero by filling out certain fields and depending on how those fields were filled out, the app would select a corresponding image to match the chosen options. The user would them have the ability to add individual powers as well as view them in the gallery. Should they feel like they no longer wanted the hero, they also had the ability to delete them entirely..
